I'm a product manager originally from Southern California, with a love of learning, data, and Netflix.

I believe that product design is an incredibly powerful tool in communication, where, if successful, it can be invisible. I love the field because there is and will always be an incredible amount to learn as products evolve to suit our needs.

My interests lie in data-based product management for consumer-focused products. I'm incredibly fascinated with uberfication and e-learning principles as they open previously non-existent opportunities for people all over the world, regardless of age, race, gender or income.

Some day, I would like to backpack the Inca Trail, get my barbour's license, and start my own company.

I just graduated from UC Irvine's School of Information and Computer Science with a B.S. degree in Informatics, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction.

Since June 2015, I've been working as a consultant at a Fortune-100 Networking company in San Jose and living in San Francisco.
