Tristan is a consultant at Capgemini in the custom software development business unit. Currently, he is at Cisco working as a Technical Project Manager to develop an Alpha Platform for Cisco Products.

Tristan started as a Product Analyst at a startup before accepting a consultant role. He comes from a Human-Computer Interaction background and constantly validates work taken on against vealue generated for the end user.

Tristan loves to ponder the big question "am I creating value?" He finds the best way for him to provide that is to help facilitate the work of other people way smarter than him through process. He is relentlessly pursuing best processes and is trying to organize them for others to leverage on pursuitofprocess.com. He enjoys writing about processes commonly found encountered as a project manager, product manager, or product analyst.

He lives on the mysterious side of SF. Some day, I would like to backpack the Inca Trail, get my barbour's license, and start my own company.
